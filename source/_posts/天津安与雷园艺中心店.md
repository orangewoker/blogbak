   若把温室视作能够有序调控光、温度、湿度以及风，且为花草提供生长环境的所在。那么以温室为依托展开售卖活动的花园中心，就能够被想象成是捕捉了光影和自然秩序的空间。

  If the greenhouse is regarded as a place that can orderly regulate light, temperature, humidity and wind, and provide a growing environment for flowers and plants. Then the garden center that conducts sales activities based on the greenhouse can be imagined as a space that captures light, shadow and natural order.
