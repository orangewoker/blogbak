遂宁店是一个兼具鲜花绿植、造景、婚纱摄影、咖啡轻食、休闲沙龙一体的复合空间。

The Suining store is a complex space integrating flowers and green plants, landscaping, wedding photography, coffee and light food, and leisure salons.
